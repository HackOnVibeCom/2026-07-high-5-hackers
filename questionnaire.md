# HackOnVibe — Project Questionnaire

**1. What does your application/service do?**

LaunchPilot AI is an AI-assisted growth cockpit for newly launched mobile apps. Describe your app once and it generates your App Store metadata and social launch copy, scores your launch readiness in real time, surfaces ranked growth recommendations, matches you with communities and influencers, and lets you plan, simulate, and publish campaigns — all from one workspace, with a context-aware AI assistant one click away on every screen.

**2. Who is the target audience?**

Founders of newly launched mobile apps — supporting them "from launch day to your first 10,000 users."

**5. What is your advantage?**

Three differentiators: (1) Always-on AI, key-optional — every AI endpoint pairs an LLM (via OpenRouter) with a deterministic, context-aware fallback engine, so the product works end to end with zero setup and nothing breaks mid-demo. (2) Human-in-the-loop — every AI draft stays locked until you approve it, and publishing goes through a visible queue (no rogue posting). (3) It closes the loop — the Growth Advisor tells you what happened, why, and the exact next action, and deep-links you to the screen where you can take it.
